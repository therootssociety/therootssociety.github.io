The Roots of
The Roots.

The Roots is an initiative which provides a space for creative representation of emotions through art. It aims at building an alternative channel free from the routinized social stagnancy, making minds imagine. It not only promotes all art forms but also validates the artist and his/her expression. The Roots foundations itself as an emanation of performing arts and thereby elongates its vision to performitivity and theatrics, encovering the realm of the corporate, holding the work experience with different academic institutions, theatre groups and companies. Implicating the key of four E’s- Experiment, Explore, Experience, Express- it delves into the inner self of a professional to accomplish the outer, strengthening the learning and the consequent growth of individual(s).

Corporate
